*Applicable to all users.*

### 1.1 Installation (Progressive Web App)
OnlyFounders is designed as a PWA to provide a native app-like experience. For full functionality (offline access, notifications, haptic feedback), you must install it on your device.

**For iOS (iPhone/iPad):**
1. Open the application link in **Safari**.
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with an arrow).
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Tap **Add**.

**For Android:**
1. Open the application link in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **Menu icon** (three dots ‚ãÆ in the top right).
3. Select **"Install App"** or **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Confirm by tapping **Install**.

### 1.2 Registration & Login
- **New Users**: Tap **APPLY NOW** on the login screen. Fill in your personal details and strictly select your specific **College/University** from the dropdown.
- **Existing Users**: Enter your registered email and password to log in.
- **Password Recovery**: If you cannot remember your password, tap **"FORGOT CREDENTIALS?"**. A secure reset link will be sent to your email address.

## üéì Chapter 2: The Founder (Student) Guide
*Your goal: Form a squad, build a product, and submit your pitch.*

### 2.1 The Dashboard
Upon logging in, you will see your "HQ."
- **Membership Card**: Your digital identity for the hackathon.
- **Countdown**: Live timer showing exactly how much time is left for submission.
- **Schedule & Tasks**: A checklist of mandatory actions and the event timeline.
- **Alerts**: Tap the **Bell Icon** (top right) to read announcements from admins.

### 2.2 Team Management
You cannot compete alone. You must either lead a team or join one.

**Option A: Creating a Team (Leader)**
1. Navigate to the **Team** tab (bottom navigation bar).
2. Tap **"Create Team"**.
3. Enter a unique **Startup Name**.
4. **Important**: The system will generate a **6-Character Access Code** (e.g., `XC9-22M`). Copy this code and share it privately with your teammates.

**Option B: Joining a Team (Member)**
1. Obtain the **Access Code** from your Team Leader.
2. Navigate to the **Team** tab.
3. Tap **"Join Team"**.
4. Enter the code. You are now synced with your squad.

### 2.3 Submitting Your Pitch
When your project is ready, go to the **Submit** tab (the center Gold button).
1. **Pitch Deck**: Upload your presentation to Google Drive. Ensure sharing settings are set to **"Anyone with the link can view"**.
2. Paste the **Google Drive link** into the app.
3. Add a brief description of your project.
4. Tap **"Submit Pitch"**.

*Note: You can edit and update your submission link up until the deadline closes.*

## üëî Chapter 3: The Admin Guide (College Ambassadors)
*Your goal: Manage your college's participation and evaluate projects.*

### 3.1 Admin Dashboard
This is your command center for your specific college.
- **Stats Overview**: View the real-time count of total teams formed, pitches submitted, and reviews pending.

### 3.2 Evaluation Process
1. Navigate to the **Submissions** tab.
2. You will see a list of teams color-coded by status:
   - üü° **Yellow**: Pending Review
   - üü¢ **Green**: Selected
   - üî¥ **Red**: Not Selected
3. Tap a **Team Card** to open their details and click the link to view their Pitch Deck.
4. **Action**: Select the appropriate status (Select, Waitlist, or Reject).
5. **Notify**: Tap **"Send Status Notification"**. This triggers an instant push notification to all members of that team regarding their result.

### 3.3 Broadcasts
Use the **Notifications** functionality within the app to send announcements (e.g., "Lunch is served," "Submission deadline extended") to all students registered under your college.
